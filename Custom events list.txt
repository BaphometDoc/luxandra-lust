This is a more in depth explanation of the available events.

==============================================
=               NEW RAID TYPES               =
==============================================

HORNY TRIBALS
This is a tribal raid that will always try to spawn from a Fantasy Race faction if the mod is active, otherwise any hostile tribal (and sometimes medieval) faction.
All the incoming raiders are rapists with increased consciousness, and when the raid arrives, a war shout will demoralize your colonists while also making them horny.

CARNAL DEVIANTS
This is a hidden faction raid that is heavily inspired by RJW's Nymphs.
All the raiders will be naked and with no weapons.
They will also all be Rapists, Zoophile, Bisexual and Nymphomaniacs.
They have a special AI that will attempt to rape your colonists as soon as there's no threats. It's kill or be raped.
And to make it all more interesting, they also come supercharged with a hediff that makes them rape machines.
Good luck.

UGLY RAPISTS (requires Unleashed Bastards)
This is a normal raid from that mod, except the raiders are supercharged with the same AI from the deviant raid.
Since these guys actually have armor and weapons, your pawns may still die, but in the event that you have to abandon some to regroup, pray for their holes, because those are about to get much wider.

==============================================
=              OTHER THREATS                 =
==============================================

RAPIST PULSE
An arcotech scream will send the highest melee skill pawn on the map on a strong rapist mental break.
If your colony is particularly big, it may be more than one pawn.

==============================================
=               MISC EVENTS                  =
==============================================

HORNY PULSE (male/female)
A one-off psychic pulse that will apply a move speed, consciousness and sex drive increase to pawns of a specific gender.

GENITAL RESIZE PULSE
A one-off psychic pulse that will apply a size increase or decrease to all penises or breasts of the humans on the map.

FERTILITY PULSE
A somewhat long lasting psychic drone will affect all pawns on the map.
Children will be confused by the strange behaviour of the adults.
All adults will gain an absolutely massive increase in fertility, in fact strong enough to overcome sterility due to old age. (planned: this can also restore ovary efficiency from menstruation)
Any kind of contraceptive will quickly lose effect. Only abstinence will prevent pregnancies... if your colonists can even manage that under the pulse's effect.
Adult males will feel stronger and faster. All the women belong to them.
Adult females will feel obsessed by their maternal instinct. Procreation isn't a choice, it's the true meaning of life.
Adult females that are already pregnant will not feel the same mating instinct, but instead will receive a massive mood boost, as they are what they should be, carriers of new life.

WHITE RAIN
It's warm. It's sticky. Maybe you shouldn't go outside today.
Pawns that walk outside will get a stacking hediff similar in concept to the Blood Rain from Anomaly.
If the hediff reaches max severity, if the pawn is a child, they will hide inside in terror. If it's an adult, they will instantly go on a rapist break.
Animals and mechs are not affected (but will probably be confused nontheless)

LUSTFUL DEPRAVITY (Ideology and Royalty variant)
Your ideology leader or your highest rank royal will gain a size decrease in their penis or breasts, become permanently nymphomaniac and rapists, get pissed for a while, and go on a rapist mental break to boot! Gods have mercy...
